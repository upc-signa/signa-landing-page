Communication without Barriers: Discover Signa, the Platform that Translates Sign Language in Real Time

Register

At Signa, we are committed to making communication more inclusive and accessible for everyone. Our platform allows people with hearing impairments to create virtual meetings and share their cameras to communicate using sign language. Thanks to our innovative technology, gestures made during video calls are automatically translated into text messages, facilitating seamless communication between hearing and deaf people. With Signa, we break down communication barriers and bring everyone together in a single digital space.

¿How It Works?

Our inclusive video calling platform uses computer vision to recognize and translate sign language in real time. When a hearing-impaired person communicates using signs during the meeting, Signa interprets their gestures and automatically converts them into text visible to other participants. Everything happens smoothly and naturally, making communication accessible, immediate, and barrier-free.
